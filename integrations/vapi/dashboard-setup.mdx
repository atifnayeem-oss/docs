---

Add pay-by-phone to a Vapi agent in a few clicks. Ringup attaches one payment tool and hands you one prompt line to paste. No payment code.

You do this **twice**: in **Test** first, on a copy of your agent, then in **Live** on your real agent. Working on a copy keeps your production agent untouched while you test.

## Before you start

<Steps>
  <Step title="A Vapi agent with a SIP-capable number">
    Vapi-native and SIP trunk (BYOC) numbers can transfer to the payment line. A number imported from a telephony provider cannot.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Your Vapi API key">
    Vapi dashboard, **Org settings, then API Keys**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Your Ringup account">
    Sign in at [ringup.dev/dashboard](https://ringup.dev/dashboard).
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Test wires a **copy** of your agent. You duplicate it in Step 1 below, so your production agent stays untouched until you deliberately go live.
</Warning>

## Set it up in Test

Make sure the dashboard is in **Test** mode (top-right toggle).

<Steps>
  <Step title="Duplicate your agent">
    In Vapi, duplicate your live agent and name the copy something like **Agent (Ringup test)**. You will wire this copy, not your production agent.

    <Frame caption="Vapi: duplicate your agent and name the copy Agent (Ringup test).">
      <img src="/images/vapi/04-duplicate-agent.png" alt="Duplicate the Vapi agent for testing" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect your platform">
    In the Ringup dashboard, open **Voice integrations**, choose **Vapi**, and paste your **Private API key**. Ringup lists your agents to check the key, then creates one payment tool on your account.

    Then pick what your agent sends at hand-off:
    - **The order id** it creates in the point of sale. The total is read from that order, so tax and modifiers are always right and the payment lands on the real ticket.
    - **The total** it works out itself. For agents with no point-of-sale order.
    - **Both**, for a mixed fleet. Each call sends whichever it has; when an order id is present it wins.

    <Frame caption="Voice integrations: choose Vapi, paste your key, pick what the agent sends.">
      <img src="/images/vapi/01-connect-vapi.png" alt="Connect Vapi and choose the order model" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose the agent">
    Pick your **test copy** and click **Add payment**. Ringup attaches one tool to it and changes nothing else.

    <Frame caption="Pick the test copy and click Add payment.">
      <img src="/images/vapi/02-pick-agent.png" alt="Choose the agent and add payment" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add the prompt line">
    Copy the one **system prompt** line Ringup shows and paste it into the test copy's system prompt in Vapi. That is the whole change on your side.

    <Frame caption="Copy the prompt line into your Vapi agent's system prompt.">
      <img src="/images/vapi/03-prompt-line.png" alt="Paste the Ringup prompt line into the Vapi agent" />
    </Frame>

    <Warning>
      Do not add the tool by hand in Vapi. Ringup attaches it with the right server URL and secret. A hand-made tool is not bound to your account and will fail at call time.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect a test merchant">
    The call can only take payment for a merchant that has connected a processor, so connect one in **Test** before you call. In the dashboard, open **Merchant integrations**, create a one-time connect link (single use, expires in ten minutes), open it, and connect a **sandbox** Square or Clover account. Without this, the payment layer stays inaudible and the test call has nothing to charge. See [Merchant integrations](/payment-providers/overview).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Place a test call">
    Call the test copy and place an order. It charges the sandbox merchant you just connected. Pay with the test card, **Visa ending 5858**; a first-time caller is texted a link and enters `4111 1111 1111 1111`. No real money moves. Watch it land in **Logs**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Go live

<Steps>
  <Step title="Wire your real agent">
    Switch the dashboard to **Live** and run **Connect your platform, Choose the agent, Add the prompt line** again, this time on your **production** agent. No copy this time.

    <Frame caption="Switch to Live and wire your production agent the same way.">
      <img src="/images/vapi/05-go-live.png" alt="Go live" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Let your merchants connect">
    Real calls take payment only for merchants who have connected their own processor. Embed the one-line Connect button so each merchant self-connects Square or Clover. Until a merchant connects, the payment layer stays inaudible on their calls, so going live first is safe. See [Merchant integrations](/payment-providers/overview).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What Ringup guarantees during the transfer

<Check>
**The hand-off is never silent.** Before the leg switches, Ringup speaks a heads-up in your agent's turn ("Just a sec, connecting you to our secure payment line now") and holds the transfer until the sentence finishes.
</Check>

<Check>
**A dropped session reference cannot strand a payment.** The Checkout Session rides the transfer two ways at once: in the transfer itself and in a short-lived server-side record keyed to the caller's number. If either carrier fails, the other completes the payment.
</Check>

<Check>
**The confirmation is pushed, not polled.** The moment a payment settles, whether by saved card on the call or a texted link paid mid-call, the payment line announces it instantly.
</Check>
